About me

I am a Ph.D. candinate (BA17011, advised by Prof. Xiaoping Chen) in the USTC Robotics Laboratory at at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received a Bachelor of Engineering from Sichuan University in 2010. My research focuses on service robot task planning in dynamic changes and uncertainties, and interests include Knowledge Representation, Computer Vision and Calibration.
